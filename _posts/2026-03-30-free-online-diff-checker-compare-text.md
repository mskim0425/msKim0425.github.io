## FAQ

**Can I compare entire files?**
Yes. Paste full file contents on both sides. Handles thousands of lines.

**Is there a line limit?**
No hard limit. Performance depends on your browser, but modern browsers handle large texts well.

**Can I compare binary files?**
No, text only. For binary diffs, use a tool like Beyond Compare.

**How is this different from `git diff`?**
Visual, color-coded output in the browser. Useful when you're not on your dev machine or just need a quick comparison.
