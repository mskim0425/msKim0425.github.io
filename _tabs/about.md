## Hey, I'm Minsub 👋

Backend developer by day, food hunter by weekend, and a curious soul trying to make sense of tech, culture, and everything in between.

I've been building things with **Java, Spring, Ruby on Rails**, and whatever else gets the job done. This blog started as a place to dump my dev notes — transaction isolation levels, design patterns, Kafka configs, you name it — but honestly, I got tired of only talking to myself about code.

### So here's the deal.

**SoThoughtful;** is where I write about:

- **Dev Life** — Real-world backend stuff. Not textbook theory, but the messy, "why-is-this-not-working" kind of notes that actually help.
- **Korean Food Adventures** — I live in Seoul. I eat well. I'll show you where and why, with photos and honest reviews. No sponsored fluff.
- **K-Culture & Trends** — What's blowing up on Korean YouTube, what's weird, what's wonderful. A window into Korean life from someone actually living it.
- **MBTI & Lifestyle** — Because apparently everyone needs to know if you're an ESTJ before they'll trust your restaurant recommendations.

### Why English?

I've been writing in Korean for years, and I love it. But I want to share Korean culture and dev experiences with a wider audience. Think of this as your **Korean friend's blog** — the one who sends you restaurant pins and debugging tips at 2am.
